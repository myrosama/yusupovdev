Active Research Project

alfaSAT

Satellite communication and monitoring system for reliable data transmission in remote areas.

The Problem

Remote areas in Central Asia often lack reliable internet connectivity. IoT devices, weather stations, and monitoring systems need a way to transmit data even when traditional networks are unavailable.

The Solution

alfaSAT is a satellite communication system designed for low-bandwidth, high-reliability data transmission. It enables IoT devices to send telemetry data via satellite, ensuring connectivity in the most remote locations.

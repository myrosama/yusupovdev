Overview

ALFASAT is an online exam platform for the new Digital SAT, enabling learning centres and students to conduct high-fidelity practice tests. The platform incorporates an automated scoring engine based on official College Board raw-to-scaled conversion tables, providing instant domain and skill-level breakdowns.

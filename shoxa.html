SHOXA

A hyperlocal discovery platform that connects people to nearby businesses, services, and experiences. Its mission is to make every small business visible by placing it on an interactive map.

Visit Website GitHub Telegram Instagram

The Problem

Uzbekistan has roughly 400,000 shops and local businesses that are "digitally ghosted"‚Äîthey do not appear on maps or directories. Finding them often requires trawling through Telegram channels or Instagram pages, with no way to check if they're open, see their products, or get directions.

The Solution

SHOXA places every business on an interactive map and provides a digital encyclopedia with detailed profiles. Users can discover cafes, shops, and services, view real-time status, read reviews, message owners directly, and get directions‚Äîall in one app.
